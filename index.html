Educational Journey

In 2019, I successfully completed my studies (Grade 12), marking the beginning of my educational journey. However, my path quickly took an unexpected turn. In 2020, while studying electrical engineering at a university, I made a bold decision to change majors. Driven by my passion for web development and software engineering, I decided to chart a new path. It was a pivotal moment that paved the way for my journey into the world of coding and technology.

A Leap of Faith

2020 brought unexpected challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic. With determination and resilience, I began my journey of teaching myself coding during quarantine. Coding became not only a skill I learned, but also a part of my daily life. It was during this time that I discovered the magic of channeling energy, realizing that the enthusiasm and energy I put into my coding was reciprocated by the incredible projects that I created.

Sales Experience

In 2021, I ventured into the world of sales, working as a salesperson in call centers for two years. Although this experience was valuable, my heart is still in the world of technology and software development.

Mobile Application Development

The year 2022 saw a significant change in my educational journey. I signed up for an online course at MTN Academy of Business Applications. For three months, I delved into the field of mobile application development. This experience expanded my skills and enriched my understanding of the ever-changing technology landscape.

The Dream Unfolds

In March 2023, I made a life-changing decision. Fueled by my unwavering passion for coding, I left my sales representative role and took a big step toward my dream. Life Choices Coding Academy, recognizing my dedication and potential, gave me a golden opportunity to hone my coding skills. Here, I immersed myself in the complexities of software development, thereby enhancing my abilities and honing my skills.

I am a go-getter with extremely precise focus when it comes to completing a task. Coding isn't just a skill I have; It's the language I speak. Basically, I am the manifestation of enthusiasm, determination, and a constant love for the ever-changing world of coding.
